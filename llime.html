My Role

For LLime, I played a key role in the second iteration of their landing page as part of a collaborative team. My responsibilities focused on refining the visual and interaction design to align with LLime’s innovative SaaS offerings and AI-driven sales solutions. I contributed to crafting a cohesive and modern user interface, ensuring an engaging and seamless experience for visitors. Additionally, I helped establish a polished design framework to maintain consistency across the brand’s digital presence.

The Problem

LLime's original landing page design was outdated and no longer effectively represented their cutting-edge SaaS platform specializing in AI-driven sales solutions. The challenge was to redesign the landing page to reflect their innovative technology  and modern ethos

The Results

The redesigned landing page successfully reflected LLime's innovative and modern ethos, creating an engaging digital experience that resonated with their target audience. The updated design elevated LLime's presence in the SaaS and AI-driven sales industry, positioning them as a trusted and cutting-edge solution provider. The refined visual identity and interaction design not only enhanced user engagement but also ensured scalability, allowing the brand to maintain consistency as it grows and adapts to future needs.
