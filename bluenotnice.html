overview
BlueNotNice approached me with the challenge of creating a unique website that could reflect the depth and unconventional nature of his portrait work. Our goal was to design a site that not only showcased the distinctiveness of his artistic vision but also delivered an immersive, user-centric experience that resonated with art enthusiasts and potential clients.
View Site
View Site

My Role

I managed every aspect of the visual, interaction design, and development process for BlueNotNice, working closely with him to ensure the final product aligned with his artistic vision. This involved developing a cohesive and bold brand identity that reflected his unique and creative approach to photography. I designed and developed the website’s visual and interactive elements to showcase his portfolio in an engaging and immersive way, while also establishing a comprehensive design system to maintain consistency across all brand touchpoints.

The Problem

BlueNotNice needed a strong digital presence for his brand in order to  captivate his audience. The challenge was to design a showcase website that reflected his bold, creative vision while creating a foundation for a memorable viewer experience.

Design System

I independently developed and managed the design system for BlueNotNice, ensuring consistency and scalability across his brand and digital presence. By creating a comprehensive style guide with reusable components, I streamlined the design process and established the foundation for a visually cohesive and high-performing website that effectively showcases his photography.

The Results

The final website successfully captured BlueNotNice’s bold and creative vision, delivering an engaging digital experience that resonated with his audience. The cohesive brand identity and striking design elevated his digital presence . The scalable design system ensured future adaptability, allowing his brand to grow while maintaining consistency across all touchpoints.
